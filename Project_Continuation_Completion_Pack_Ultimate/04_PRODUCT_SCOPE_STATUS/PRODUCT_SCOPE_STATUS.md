# PRODUCT_SCOPE_STATUS

## Original scope
Browser Extension, Web Dashboard, Mobile Companion App, Digital DNA Scan, Identity Builder, Attention Alignment Score, Feed Simulator, Interest Evolution Engine, Discovery Mode, Weekly AI Reflection and Personal Growth Dashboard.

## Current scope
Runnable local MVP scaffold with dashboard, scoring, demo scan, feed simulator, weekly reflection, API, PWA manifest and extension scaffold.

## What scope expanded
Continuation docs, source-of-truth management and handoff artifacts have been added.

## What scope should be reduced
Do not build full mobile native app, full recommendation integration or large AI automation before persistence/auth/consent are settled.

## What is still strategically important
Identity/focus model, Digital DNA ingestion, alignment scoring, actionable reflection, consented browser capture and privacy controls.

## What no longer matters
Template placeholders and old scaffold assumptions that do not match the current Node/static architecture.
