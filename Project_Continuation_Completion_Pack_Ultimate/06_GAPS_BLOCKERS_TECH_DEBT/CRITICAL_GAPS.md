# CRITICAL_GAPS

## Gap 1
Persistence is missing.

## Gap 2
Authentication and authorization are missing.

## Gap 3
Consent, privacy export/delete and retention rules are missing.

## Why each gap matters
AttentionOS handles potentially sensitive attention and browsing-derived data. Without durable storage, identity, and privacy controls, it cannot safely serve real users.

## What happens if it is ignored
Data can be lost, mixed between users, collected without valid consent, or become impossible to delete/export reliably.
